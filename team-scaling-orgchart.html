CEO / CTO / CPO — Scope & Responsibilities

Clear definition of responsibilities, interactions, and boundaries to eliminate gray areas in day-to-day execution.

CEO — Valentin BARTHEL

Chief Executive Officer · €120K · Scope: Strategy, M&A, Sage Executive Relations

Core responsibilities

  • Strategic vision and market positioning
  • Sage executive relations (sponsors, steering committee)
  • M&A, fundraising and capital structure
  • Governance and board reporting
  • C-Level recruitment and company culture
  • Global P&L and budget arbitration

Key interactions

  • CTO: Tech/business roadmap alignment
  • CPO: Product priority validation, go-to-market
  • Head of Growth: Pipeline and revenue targets
  • Sage: Executive sponsorship, co-development
  • Board: Quarterly reporting, strategic decisions

AI tools used

  • Claude/GPT for strategic analysis and writing
  • AI dashboards for real-time KPI monitoring
  • Automated reporting and board preparation

CTO — Benjamin ROUVILLE

Chief Technology Officer · €100K · Scope: Architecture, Tech Team, Quality, Infrastructure

Core responsibilities

  • Technical architecture and technology decisions
  • Engineering team management (up to 7 people)
  • Code quality: review, standards, CI/CD
  • Cloud infrastructure, security, 99.9% SLA
  • Technical roadmap and tech debt management
  • AI technology watch and integrations (LLMs, ML pipelines)

Builder → Leader transition

  • M1-M6: 60% hands-on code / 40% management
  • M6-M12: 40% code / 60% architecture & leadership
  • M12+: 20% code / 80% leadership & tech strategy

Tech team (Phase 3)

  • Cloud/DevOps Engineer — Infra & CI/CD
  • QA/Reliability Engineer — Testing & quality
  • Full-Stack Developer #1 — Core features
  • Full-Stack Developer #2 — Integrations
  • Sr. Backend/Data Engineer — Data pipelines
  • Platform/Security Engineer — SOC2, security

CTO / CPO boundary

  • CTO: How we build (technical)
  • CPO: What we build (functional)
  • CTO proposes technical solutions
  • CPO validates user impact

CPO — Jean RICHARD DE VESVROTTE

Chief Product Officer · €100K · Scope: Product, UX, CS, Analytics, Partner Enablement

Core responsibilities

  • Product vision and functional roadmap
  • UX/UI design and user experience
  • Customer Success and retention (NRR > 120%)
  • Product analytics and data-driven decisions
  • Partner enablement and documentation
  • Pricing and packaging

CPO team (Phase 3)

  • Customer Success Lead — CS strategy, key accounts
  • CS Ops/Onboarding — Operations, L1 support

90-day plan — Jean

  • D1-D30: Product immersion, top 10 client meetings, full UX audit, NPS baseline
  • D30-D60: Product roadmap V1, analytics setup (Mixpanel/Amplitude), first UX quick wins
  • D60-D90: Structured CS process, automated onboarding V1, first review with metrics (activation, retention)

Detailed CPO / CTO boundary

  • CPO owns: Functional specs, prioritization, UX, analytics
  • CTO owns: Architecture, performance, security, infra
  • Shared zone: API design, frontend framework choices, data model
  • Arbitration: CEO decides on persistent disagreements
